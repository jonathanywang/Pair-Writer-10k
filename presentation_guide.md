# Presentation guide

1. Individual / Team Introductions
   - Introduce who is on the team
   - Introduce your team name
2. What is your project?
   - What does your project do?
   - What problem does your project solve?
   - Who will be using your project?
3. Demo your project
   - Prepare a few prompts as a backup plan
   - Prepare what you're going to say as the LLM is thinking
4. Choose at least two of the following:
   - Talk about the coolest part of the project
   - Talk about the most challenging part of your project
   - Talk about a funny moment from the project
   - Talk about something you learned
   - Talk about something you would add if you had more time
